Bubbles — Progress

Bubbles is a personal AI agent built to serve one person across a lifetime. She handles daily tasks, anticipates needs before they're asked, guides through trade-offs honestly, watches over wellbeing and privacy, and represents her person in delegated contexts when authorized.

What she learns about the person she serves belongs to that person — not behind a corporate login that can be revoked or repurposed. She's built to outlast any single AI model, platform, or company.
